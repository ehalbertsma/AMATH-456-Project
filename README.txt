# To add citations:

1. go to google scholar
2. search the paper/book that you need
3. when you see the right result on google scholar, click on the blue quote marks at the bottom of the listing
4. click BibTex
5. copy and paste the BibTex citation into the ref.bib file in Overleaf
6. in order to cite, type:
\cite{} and you'll see your citation in the suggestions
